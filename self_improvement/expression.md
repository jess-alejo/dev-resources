## Expression

#### brass tacks

- to get down to brass tacks with to talk about important facts

#### kick it to the curb

- to dismiss something

#### lo and behold

- an expression used to show wonder

#### old wive's tale

- a common belief that's not based on fact

#### one and the same

- identical

#### part and parcel

- an essential element

#### razed to the ground

- destroyed completely

#### whet your appetite

- to increase desire
